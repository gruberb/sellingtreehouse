Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a condo or a co-op?

This is a registered condominium (LCCC No. 37). You own your unit with a deed, can get standard mortgage financing, and can sell it like any other condo. The only difference is your neighbors have chosen to be more intentional about building community.

What do the monthly fees include?

Your monthly condo fees include heat, electricity, water, high-speed internet, snow removal, lawn care, building maintenance, and upkeep of all shared spaces. This makes budgeting predictable‚Äîno surprise utility bills.

Are the homes really private?

Absolutely. Your home is 100% private, just like any condo. The shared spaces (Common House, workshop, etc.) are amenities you can use when you want‚Äîthink of them like a condo building's gym or party room, but much better.

Can I get a mortgage like a normal condo?

Yes! Banks treat this exactly like any other condominium because that's what it is legally. Standard financing, standard down payment requirements, standard everything.

Is the home accessible for aging in place?

Yes, this unit is ground-level with no stairs, wide doorways, a barrier-free bathroom, and turning radius for wheelchairs. It's designed so you can live here comfortably at any stage of life.

How far is it from Halifax?

About 104 km (approximately 1 hour 10 minutes drive). Close enough for city amenities when you need them, far enough for peaceful daily living.
